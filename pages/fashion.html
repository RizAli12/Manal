Company Overview

Manal is considered as one of the leading Importer and supplier of Linen products in Saudi Arabia, company also has operations in the Middle East countries, carrying out demand in the Hospitality industries and Textiles business. Through our wide experience since 2000 our expert staff have got the knowledge how to provide our valued customers and business partners with the best special made fabrics to serve the hospitality industry with all its requirements and needs of the field of uniforms, bedding, Towels and table cloths. Our logistic department consistently takes the recent methodology in R&D to provide us with the fine rethread to specially produce most of our fabrics with our special formulas for dying and preparation and treated against the defects of sun, sweat, shrinkage, color fading and when required (if any) against flame and/or grease to enable us to provide our customers with one year warranty against manufacturing defects. Our design department is always ready to support our valued clients with all the assistance to set their direction according to their concepts, themes and color scheme. Our business Development Department during last3 years has been involved in many development plans in many marketing Exhibitions in Saudi Arabia and Middle East.
